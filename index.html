What is dau?

dau is an integrated hardware/software platform for accelerating streaming computations

Composable cores

dau contains a large number of composable compute cores, from the simplest filtering operations to the most complicated streaming joins

Extremely fast

dau is optimized for high-throughput, latency sensitive deployments

Flexible

The dau engine can be deployed on most modern hardware, deployable on FPGS targets ranging from hobbyist tier through enterprise
